LEARN HOW TO FOLD

Step by Step Origami Instructions and Diagrams

TUTORIAL

BOAT

Step 1: Start with a rectangular piece of paper, colored side up. Fold in half, then open.



Step 2: Fold in half downwards.



Step 3: Bring corners in to center line.



Step 4: Fold uppermost layer upwards & do the same to the back. Crease well.



Step 5: Pull the sides out and flatten.



Step 6: Fold front layer up to top, & do the same to the back.



Step 7: Pull the sides outwards and flatten.



Step 8: Gently pull the top parts of the model outwards, making a boat shape.



Step 9: Flatten well to crease all folds. Then open out slightly, forming a boat or hat shape. Finished!
