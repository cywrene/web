Step by Step Origami Instructions and Diagrams

TUTORIAL

STRAWBERRY

Step 1: Fold in half both sides. It should have four squares now.



Step 2: Open the pocket from inside.



Step 3: Flatten it to square.



Step 4: Turn over.



Step 5: Open the pocket and flatten again like from step 2 and 3.



Step 6: Turn around.



Step 7: Fold backwards in the dotted lines.



Step 8: Fold in the dotted lines.



Step 9: Fold backwards in the dotted lines.



Step 10: Fold in the dotted lines.



Step 11: Draw seeds and finished.
