LEARN HOW TO FOLD

Step by Step Origami Instructions and Diagrams

TUTORIAL

FOX

Step 1: This is the front of the paper, our fox's mouth will be this colour.



Step 2: This is the back of the paper, our fox's ears will be this colour.



Step 3: Fold the right edge of the paper over to the left.



Step 4: Unfold the paper.



Step 5: Fold the top edge of the paper down to the bottom edge.



Step 6: Fold the right edge to align with the central vertical crease that you created in the previous step.



Step 7: Fold the left edge to meet the central crease.



Step 8: Fold the top left point of the right section diagonally down to the right.



Step 9: Unfold the previous step.



Step 10: Open out the right section.



Step 11: Flatten the right section, using the diagonal crease to create a house shape.



Step 12: Repeat the same process on the left side.



Step 13: Flip the paper over to the other side.



Step 14: Fold the right edge to the middle.



Step 15: Fold the left edge to the middle too.



Step 16: Fold the top layer of the bottom right point diagonally up to the left.



Step 17: Fold the top layer of the bottom section diagonally up, creating a crease that runs from the bottom left corner up to the top right corner of the lower section.



Step 18: Fold the front layer up to the top. This has created an ear.



Step 19: Flip the model over to the other side.



Step 20: Repeat the same process on this side, but this time it's the left side.



Step 21: Fold the bottom left point diagonal up.



Step 22: Fold the bottom section up to create the other ear.



Step 23: Rotate the model so that it's as shown.



Step 24: Pull the model open from behind and squash the mouth inwards.



Step 25: When you close the mouth completely, you will be able to straighten out the nose creases.



Step 26: The origami fox puppet is complete.
