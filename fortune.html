LEARN HOW TO FOLD

Step by Step Origami Instructions and Diagrams

TUTORIAL

FORTUNE TELLER

Step 1: Valley fold paper from corner to corner, making a triangle.



Step 2: Valley fold the triangle from corner to corner, making a smaller triangle.



Step 3: Unfold everything -- you'll have a square of paper with an X crease.



Step 4: Fold the corner to the center of the square (where the X crosses).



Step 5: Repeat with the other three corners.



Step 6: And you end up with a smaller square!



Step 7: Flip your paper over.



Step 8: Fold the corner to the center of the square (where the X crosses).



Step 9: Repeat with the other three corners and you'll end up with an even smaller square.



Step 10: Fold and unfold the bottom edge of the square up to the top.



Step 11: Fold and unfold the left edge of the square over to the right.



Step 12: This bit is easier to do than it is to explain -- basically, you want to push the four corners of the square together in the center and then slide 4 fingers into the flaps of your fortune teller, creasing the folds back so your fingers fit in nicely. You'll be able to move the flaps with your fingers like little puppets.



Step 13: Now, design and put different choices in every square. Finished!
