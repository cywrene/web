LEARN HOW TO FOLD

Step by Step Origami Instructions and Diagrams

TUTORIAL

CRAB

Step 1: Fold the paper in half.



Step 2: Fold the paper in half again.



Step 3: Open up the top flap of paper over to the right.



Step 4: Squash fold this flap flat.



Step 5: Turn the paper over.



Step 6: Open up the top flap of paper over to the left and Squash Fold it flat.



Step 7: Fold the top two flaps of paper behind along the dotted lines.



Step 8: Turn the paper over.



Step 9: Fold the bottom part of the paper up along the dotted line.



Step 10: Fold the side flaps of paper down along the dotted lines.



Step 11: Turn the paper over.



Step 12: The completed crab.
