LEARN HOW TO FOLD

Step by Step Origami Instructions and Diagrams

TUTORIAL

BANANA

Step 1: Start with a square sheet of origami paper and rotate it like a diamond. If you only have regular paper, follow instructions on how to make origami paper.



Step 2: Fold the top corner to the bottom corner.



Step 3: Fold the bottom corner slightly higher than the top edge.



Step 4: Fold the top corner behind the edge.



Step 5: Fold the right corner to the left corner, creasing at the half way point.



Step 6: Take the left corner from the top layer and fold it down to near the bottom corner.



Step 7: Now take the left corner from the remaining layer and fold it down like you see on the picture.
