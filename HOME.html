ORUKAMI

Orukami was what we commonly known as Origami where Oru means "folding" and kami means "paper". It was originated in Japan but it was now popular outside the country. The objective of this paper folding was to transform a piece of square paper into figures through applying folding techniques.

With simple instructions, diagrams and video tutorials, it lets you to easily create any figure that you desired to make. It doesn’t only end on just a figure but as well as different designs. These designs can be done by combining your origami folds in variety ways.


Requirements:
1. Paper
2. Scissor, Ruler and Pen (optional)
3. Patience
